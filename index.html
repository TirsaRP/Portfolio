About   this   project:

While working as a product developer for MyEsteeme, I was responsible for creating a whole new experience for our customers. This was a new division of the company. I began by addressing the needs of the stakeholders. Knowing the requirements of our company, of the company we were franchising to, and of our customers; parents who want to book parties outside of their home. I then conducted research on what activities were age appropriate: what the children in our target age group are capable of physically and cognitively.I then searched for activities that met the criteria. I developed a few of my own activities and repurposed others found online.

Once I knew which activities I wanted to incorporate into the program, I started developing the artefacts. I created maps, posters, masks, scavenger items, and more. Each party had a different theme, so each item needed to be specific to the particular theme and character (mascot). For example, one party had a space theme. That party package included space related games and challenges that engaged the children's fine and gross motor skills. In addition to creating enjoyable activities, they needed to be educational, as well as follow the equal treatment values of MyEsteeme.
