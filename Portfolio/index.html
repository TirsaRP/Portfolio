About this project:

While working as a product developer
I needed to create a whole new
experience for our customers.
This was a new division of
the company. I started with knowing
the requirements of our company,
of the company we were franchising to,
and of parents who want to book parties
outside of the home. Then I conducted
a lot of research of what activities
are age appropriate: what are the
children in our target age group
capable of physically and cognitively.
I then searched for which activities
met that criteria. I developed some
of my own activities and repurposed
others.

Once I knew which activities
I wanted to incorporate into
the program, I started developing
the artefacts. I created maps,
posters, masks, scavenger items,
and more. Each party had a different
theme, so each item needed to
be specific to that theme and
character (mascot).

When the first parties were booked,
I trained the employees that
would lead the parties.
Then I would observe each party,
interview the parents and the leaders
afterwards to get feedback on what
they thought worked and what
needed to be changed. There
were many iterations before we
refined our product and found
a layout that worked for
everyone.
